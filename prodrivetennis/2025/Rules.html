ProDrive Tennis

Match Location

  • Matches are to be played around Natick preferably.
  • Both the playing teams needs to coordinate on the location, time and play the match.
  • This is a friendly tournament and the organizers don't reserve any courts for the match.

Start Time

  • The start time for matches is 6pm on the day scheduled, unless other arrangements are made. If a team does not show up by 6:30pm or 30mins after the aggreed time, a forfeit may be taken.

Match Format and Scoring System

  • Matches are best two out of three sets.
  • Points: 15, 30, 40, Game. Games make sets, sets make matches.
