2025 Tournament Rules

Match Location

  • - Location: Matches should preferably be played in or around Natick.
  • - Coordination: Both teams are responsible for coordinating and agreeing upon the match time and location.
  • - Court Booking: Please note that this is a friendly tournament; organizers will not reserve courts. Teams are responsible for securing their own court.

Start Time

  • - Start Time: The default start time for all matches is 6:00 PM on the scheduled day.
  • - Rescheduling: Teams may mutually agree to a different start time.
  • - Forfeit Rule: A forfeit will be enforced if a team fails to appear within 30 minutes of the scheduled start time (i.e., by 6:30 PM for a 6:00 PM start) or within 30 minutes of the alternatively agreed-upon time.

Match Format and Scoring System

  • - Match Format: All matches are best two out of three sets.
  • - Scoring - Points: Points are scored as 15, 30, 40, Game.
  • - Scoring - Games: A player must win at least four points and have a two-point lead to win a game (e.g., 40-advantage, then next point wins).
  • - Scoring - Sets: To win a set, a player must win at least six games and have a two-game lead (e.g., 6-4).
  • - Set Tie-break: If a set reaches a score of 6-6, a standard tie-break will be played. The first player to reach seven points with a two-point lead wins the tie-break and the set (e.g., 7-6).
  • - Match Tie-break (Super Set / Third Set Option):
    • -- If the match score reaches one set all (1-1), a super tie-break (first to 10 points with a two-point lead) will be played to determine the match winner.
    • -- However, if both teams mutually agree to play a full third set, the standard set scoring practice (first to six games with a two-game lead, with a tie-break at 6-6) will apply for that third set.
